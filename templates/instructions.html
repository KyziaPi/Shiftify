Hi, We Are Shiftify!

Shiftify is a python sliding puzzle game created by Gueco, Yerika Elaine S., Aquino, Katrine Ingrid O., and Villanueva, Anne Beatriz M.. We are 2nd year college student from Angeles University Foundation under the College of Computer Studies. It was developed as her final requirement for CS50x, showcasing her skills and understanding about python, javascript, and HTML.

How to Play?

1. Chose a Mode: Select from the three difficulty levels: Easy (3x3) Normal (4x4) Hard (5x5)

2. Goal: Arrange the tiles in a numerical order by sliding them into the empty space.

3. Move Tiles: Click on the tiles to move it to the empty space.

4. Win: Solve the puzzle by arranging the tiles correctly.

TOP THE LEADERBOARD: Only top 10 will be shown! There are two types of leaderboards per difficulty, one for least number of tiles moved and another for least amount of time!
